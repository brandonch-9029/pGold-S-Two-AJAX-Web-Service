Why should you study Art?

In the words of Leo Tolstoy:

"The activity of art is based on the capacity of people to infect others with their own emotions and to be infected by the emotions of others. Strong emotions, weak emotions, important emotions or irrelevant emotions, good emotions or bad emotions – if they contaminate the reader, the spectator, or the listener – it attains the function of art."

A beginner's guide to Art History

This website aims to be a simple primer on the profound evolution and development of art over the centuries.
It is my hope that the pieces introduced here can pique your interest and help you foster a deeper appreciation for art.

Art historians typically consider the Romanesque art era to be the start of the art history timeline. Romanesque art developed during the rise of Christianity ca. 1000 AD. During this time, only a small percentage of the European population were literate, so paintings of Christian objects, stories, saints and ceremonies were used to spread Christianity.

Master of Taüll

The Master of Taüll is considered the greatest mural painter of the 12th century in Catalonia, as well as one of the most important Romanesque painters in Europe.
